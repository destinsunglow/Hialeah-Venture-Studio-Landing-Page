§ 08 / FAQ

Questions founders ask.

Do I need to be enrolled in college?+
No. We take students, grads, self-taught founders, international applicants, and working engineers. The only admission criterion is proof of build — photos, video, code, a Fusion file.
What does 5% equity actually cover?+
Everything. 60 days of facility access, the Ghost Lab crew, $1M+ of equipment, on-site housing, the Demo Day reel, the investor intros, and lifetime alumni access. No hidden fees, no tuition, no cash.
What if I don't have a co-founder?+
Apply solo. We run a co-founder match process in the two weeks before cohort start. About 30% of our teams form on-site.
I'm an international student. Does this work?+
Yes. We support F-1 CPT/OPT, J-1, and O-1 pathways. On acceptance we issue an invitation letter and connect you with our immigration partner. Miami is one of the most international-friendly cities in the country.
Do I keep my IP?+
Yes. The startup owns all IP. The studio takes 5% common equity in the startup — standard cap table, same Delaware C-corp mechanics as YC.
What happens if we don't finish in 60 days?+
Most teams don't finish. The goal is a working prototype and a pitch — not a shipping product. Standout teams get extension contracts (more time, more budget, sometimes a check) on Demo Day night.
Can I apply with just an idea?+
No. You need proof of build — a past project, a personal rig, a GitHub, a prototype video, something you've made. The idea can be brand new. The builder can't be.
Is software allowed?+
Only as the brain of a hardware product. Pure SaaS / consumer apps / agents belong at YC or Techstars. We build atoms.
